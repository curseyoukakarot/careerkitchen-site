Frequently Asked Questions

What is RecruitPro?

RecruitPro is a step-by-step training and mentorship program that teaches you how to break into tech recruiting as a freelancer — even if you’ve never worked in hiring before.

Do I need a tech background?

Not at all. Many of our most successful students come from customer service, sales, HR, or completely unrelated fields. We teach you everything you need to know from the ground up.

How do I make money?

You earn commission for every candidate you place with a client. RecruitPro shows you how to source, pitch, and close clients — and how to get your first placements fast.

Do you offer support?

Yes! You’ll get access to a private Slack community, weekly coaching sessions, client pitch templates, and direct support from our team when you’re stuck.
